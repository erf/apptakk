# Cashplan — Account Deletion

## How to delete your account

Open Cashplan, tap the menu in the top left and choose **Account**. At the bottom of the page tap the red **Delete account** button. Confirm in the dialog that follows.

## What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your account record and all associated data: plans, categories, and amounts.
