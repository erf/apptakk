'Cashplan - easy budget' account deletion policy

How to delete account

Open the 'Cashplan - easy budget' app and in the top left menu choose Account. At the bottom of the page there is a red 'Delete account' button which deletes your account and all related data. An alert dialog will prompt for additional confirmation. Press 'Delete' to confirm.

What data is deleted

In the case of account deletion - all related data to the users account is deleted. This includes: account, user data, plans and amounts.
