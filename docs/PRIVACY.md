# Privacy policy

Last updated Oct 2, 2020.

It is AppTakk's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our app, Cashplan.

In this Privacy policy, we seek to explain what information we collect, how we use it and what your rights are. 

If there are any terms you do not agree with, please stop using the app.

We may update this notice.

### What we collect

We collect data you provide us when using the app. This is data related to plans, categories, amounts, etc. We store data using Cloud Firestore. 

We collect location data if you allow it, in order to show the location of amounts. We use Google Maps SDK in order to show a map of your amounts.

We use Firebase Authentication to sign you in. You are able to sign in using your Google account, AppleID and Phone.

We use Google Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to log usage and crashes. 

You can read more about Google privacy policies here:
https://firebase.google.com/terms
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

And about Apple privacy policies here:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy

### Your rights

You are able to delete, edit and (soon) export your data, via various pages in the app, like the Account page, Profile page, Plan page, Amount list page etc. 

Your data is only available to you (and us developers). We do not store data after it is deleted, and we do not share or sell your data. 

We will try our upmost to secure your data, but we make no guarantees against loss, theft or modification of data.
