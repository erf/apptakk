# Cashplan — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: May 2026*

Cashplan uses Google Firebase services to provide its core functionality:

- **Firebase Authentication** — to sign you in via Google, Apple ID, or phone. We store an account identifier so we can associate your data with you.
- **Cloud Firestore** — to store the data you create in the app (plans, categories, amounts).
- **Firebase Crashlytics** — to receive crash reports so we can fix bugs.
- **Google Analytics for Firebase** — to understand aggregate usage patterns.
- **Google Maps API** — to show map-related features.

We only store the data needed to provide the service, and we do not sell or share it with third parties except as required by law.

By signing in via Google or Apple, you also agree to:

- https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app at any time. See [account deletion](cashplan-delete.md).
